Practical support

Built for the messages that matter

CoParent Compass is designed for the everyday exchanges separated parents need to manage: handovers, school updates, schedule changes, expenses, and boundaries. It supports calmer communication, clearer direction, and better organisation during emotionally complex moments.

Calmer communication

Refine difficult messages into clear, respectful communication centred on the child's needs.

Organised records

Keep key messages, arrangements, and shared decisions structured and easy to return to.

Steadier decisions

Add a calmer pause before responding, so the next step is considered and proportionate.

Record keeping

Keep important communication easier to follow

Co-parenting can create many messages, notes, receipts, and small decisions. CoParent Compass will help make that information clearer, more organised, and easier to revisit when parents need context.

Handovers Dates, times, location notes
Arrangements School, holidays, appointments
Costs Shared expenses and receipts

Product principles

Responsible support for real family moments

Plain language, calm prompts, and practical support for difficult exchanges.

AI-supported tools designed to assist, not decide or advise.

Organisation that helps parents understand what has been agreed.

Privacy, safety, and trust considered before sensitive features are added.
